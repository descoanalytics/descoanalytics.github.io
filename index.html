About us


We are a forward-looking, data-driven energy analytics company delivering intelligent energy
management advisory services through connecting human experience with machine learning.

We believe energy analytics should be automated, effortless, affordable, traceable, rewarding and beautiful.

Simple

You have big data. We give you actionable insights through automated data mining and streaming analytics. Transparent, traceable.

Secure

All of our your data is secured and all of our recommendations are validated by third parties. You have fine grain control.

Beautiful

Save time through our intelligent queries: no clutter, just the important stuff: maximum savings, maximized impact, minimal effort.

Services

Meet DEEP - the DESCO Energy Eco-pilot Platfrom
actionable insights.  automated.  proven.  verified.  transparent.  beautiful.  delivered.

Machine learning

DEEP analyses your energy vectors and looks for patterns to identify savings opportunities and conversts them into clear action points. We train our models on the largest datasets in the field.

Decision-support portal

You interact with DEEP through a beautifully crafted decision support portal. Using an intelligent query interface, it understands and answers your questions as if it were human.

Human validation

Recommendations generated by our algorithms are cross-validated by independent third parties. Once part of DEEP, you can become a validator too.

Streaming analytics

Once set up, DEEP continually monitors your data flows, re-trains and re-runs, always leading to newer insights, better forecasts and more value.
